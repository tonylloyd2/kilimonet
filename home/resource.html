MASTITIS

Mastitis is livestock disease that can be contracted by cow,sheep and goats.

symptoms of mastitis

swollen udder
tainted millk
mere touching of cow udder causes pain and discomfort to the animal.
treatment
use of Antibiotics

PARASITES

Parasites can be classified into categories name: external parasites e.g ticks and internal parasites e.g liverfluke

SYMPTOMS OF AN ANIMAL INFECTED BY PARASITES

rough coat
coughing
Stomach cramps and pain.
Nausea or vomiting.
Dehydration.
Weight loss.
Swollen lymph nodes.
Digestive problems including unexplained constipation, diarrhoea or persistent gas.
Skin issues such as rashes, eczema, hives, and itching.
Continuous muscle and joint pain

fodder and pastures management

kenya arid and semi arid land such kajiado county makes 80% of kenya landmass and home to a third of kenya population but livelihood is under threat of climate change. Climate change has led to scarcity of pasture and fodder .as a result pastoralism is severely affected .this can be can be mitigated through initiation of programs such as :
1.participatory rangeland management(PRM).
PRM encourages locals to take more active roles in land and resources management where by the land is map to identity key resources.
2.practicing rotational grazing,in rotational grazing the grazing field has to be fence then subarticleide the field into small portions.
3.keeping few number of animals to control overgrazing.This help to ensure sufficient feeds are available throughout the season.
4.hay and silage making.This helps to conserve surplus feeds for next season use
